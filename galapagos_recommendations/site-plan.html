Galapagos Reccommendations

Purpose

To create a dynamic, bilingual web platform where users can share and discover local recommendations in Galápagos—such as nature spots, restaurants, and tours.

Audience

  • Independent travelers and families looking for trusted local tips.
  • Tour guides and small business owners seeking visibility.
  • Local residents who want to share their favorite places or discover new ones.

Dinamyc Elements Used

  • Form with eventListeners for validation and submission.
  • Creation of JavaScript objects for each recommendation.
  • Storage and manipulation of data using an array of objects.
  • Dynamic rendering of cards using array.map().
  • Filtering and searching with array.filter() and array.find().
  • Real-time statistics using array.reduce() (e.g., average rating).
  • Responsive layout using CSS Grid and Media Queries.

Day Tour to Santa Fe Island and Hidden Beach with snorkeling and fishing

Swim with playful sea lions and other Galapagos marine life on a small-group snorkeling and fishing tour to Santa Fe Island. A short boat ride from Puerto Ayora takes you to a peaceful bay with crystalline waters. Using provided snorkel gear, get up close to sea turtles, eagle rays, sharks, and a kaleidoscope of tropical fish. Later, take a wildlife tour of the island and have the chance to fish during the return journey.

Day Tour to Isabela Island with Tintorera Islet, Snorkeling Wetlands & Flamingos

Embark on a thrilling day tour to Isabela Island and discover its diverse natural wonders. Begin at the Tintorera Islet, home to various marine life, including marine iguanas, sharks, and penguins. Later, using provided snorkel gear, observe the island’s underwater world up close. You’ll also visit the island’s wetlands, a habitat for flamingos, and the Giant Tortoise Breeding Centre. This excursion promises an adventurous and educational experience that’s fun for everyone
